How We Started

Our Passion is Baking Happiness

Sweet Bakery was founded by Arman Yusuf, a passionate artisan baker, and Lisa Rahma, a dessert connoisseur. Their shared dream was to bring freshly baked joy to every home. Fueled by love for flavor and quality, they created a bakery where every product is handmade with care, passion, and the finest ingredients.
